01 · About

Academic rigor meets industry impact

I am an Assistant Professor of Cybersecurity & AI at the University of Petra and Co-Founder / Head of AI at Sygma Global Software Solutions. My doctoral research at Loughborough University (UK) developed GAN-based architectures for network anomaly detection, and has been published in top-tier venues including Knowledge-Based Systems, Applied Soft Computing and IET Networks.

At Sygma, I lead the design and delivery of AI-powered products - smart ordering systems, data analytics platforms, call-center agents and healthcare AI - bringing academic depth into production-grade engineering. I also serve as Assistant Director of E-Learning and peer-review for Springer, Elsevier and Nature.
